About Us

At We Save Animals, we are dedicated to providing a safe haven for homeless animals. Our NGO passionately takes in animals from the streets, offering them shelter, care, and a second chance at a happy and healthy life. With a compassionate team of volunteers and experts, we strive to address the physical and emotional needs of each animal that comes through our doors.

Organisation

WSA believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving animal rescue their health, and survival.

Vision

we believe that every life deserves a chance. By providing a supportive and nurturing environment, we strive to make a lasting impact on the lives of homeless animals, one precious soul at a time. Join us in our mission to create a world where no animal is left behind, and together, we can make a difference in the lives of these incredible creatures.

Mission

Our dedicated staff ensures that each furry friend receives personalized attention, exercise, and mental stimulation to foster their overall well-being. We also actively work towards finding suitable adoptive families that can provide a loving and permanent home for our rescued animals.
